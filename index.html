Press Release - January 6th, 2020

Synth-pop artist MONSTER MANSION announces the release of a new EP titled VIDEO WORLD, accompanied by a VIDEO GAME of the same name.



Click to listen to the EP

VIDEO WORLD is a 3 track EP set for release on Jan 22nd, 2021. The songs were written and produced by MONSTER MANSION (aka writer/producer Jacob Leaney) in Melbourne, with mixing credit to Tristan Hoogland (Joji, Flume) and additional songwriting credit to Chris O'Neill (Metal Gear Solid).

VIDEO WORLD is accompanied by a 20 minute VIDEO GAME of the same name (available for PC & Mac via Steam and the Mac App Store) where you play as a 90's video store employee during halloween. The game juxta-poses the dark edge of the music with a comedic characters and adorable visuals.

The game was developed by Things For Humans, Leaney's own micro game studio.

Game press kit available at: https://videoworld.thingsforhumans.studio.

Bio

MONSTER MANSION is synth-pop artist and indie game developer Jacob Leaney. With clear influences from being a 90s kid and from artists like Japanese Wallpaper and Chvrches, his music is dark yet energetic. He also develops companion videogames for each releases.

MONSTER MANSION debuted with a single called 'Human Rights' in 2019 - an anthem inspired by the 2017 marriage equality vote in Australia. This release came with an interactive lyrics video.

Leaney's background as an engineer/songwriter includes working at the acclaimed SING SING STUDIOS and with producer Dean Tuza. Previous credits include Stella Donnelly, G-Flip, Jet, Tina Arena, Craig Connelly, Kate Ceberano, and The Avalanches.
