Bio

Monster Mansion is the Melbourne indie synth-pop project of writer/producer Jake Leaney.

Since Monster Mansion's debut EP release in 2021, notable achievements include singles 'Galaxies' and 'Blood Like Wine' featured in indie video games, premiering live to a sold-out live crowd at the 2022 Melbourne International Games Week, Leaney performing on Channel 10's Thank God You're Here (2023, 2024), and being a semi-finalist in the International Songwriting Competition.

Monster Mansion's debut album is being released in November 2024, and includes songwriting collaborations with Brendan Maclean, Benjamin Beamish (Jude York), queer USA-based pop-artist Myylo, and K-Pop hitmaker Alina Smith (Itzy, Red Velvet).

Leaney's background as an engineer/songwriter includes working at the acclaimed Sing Sing Studios and with producer Dean Tuza (The Rubens, Stella Donnelly). His previous production credits include Stella Donnelly, G-Flip, Jet, Tina Arena, Craig Connelly, Kate Ceberano, and The Avalanches.
